Biography and Background

Ndalo Kaphwiyo was born in Lilongwe, Malawi, and moved to Pretoria, South Africa, for high school. Growing up, Ndalo was always drawn to creativity, problem-solving, and the art of persuasion. He is currently studying Marketing and Digital Media because he loves the idea of helping brands grow and connect with their audiences. He is passionate about social media marketing, advertising strategies, and content creation. His goal is to work in brand management and one day launch his own digital marketing agency.

Hobbies

Ndalo is passionate about graphic design and social media content creation. He enjoys traveling to new places and creating vlogs about her experiences. In his free time, he also practices yoga to stay active and relieve stress.
