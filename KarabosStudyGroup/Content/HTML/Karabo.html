Biography and Background

Karabo Kaula was born in Polokwane, South Africa, and attended high school in Tzaneen. She has always been passionate about helping others and understanding human behavior. She is currently pursuing a degree in Psychology because she is deeply interested in mental health and personal development. Karabo wants to become a clinical psychologist to assist people in overcoming challenges and improving their well-being. She believes that mental health is just as important as physical health and hopes to make a positive impact in people's lives.

Hobbies

Karabo loves reading psychology books and listening to podcasts about personal growth. She also enjoys journaling and writing about mental health topics. In addition, she volunteers at a youth mental health organization, helping others with emotional support.
