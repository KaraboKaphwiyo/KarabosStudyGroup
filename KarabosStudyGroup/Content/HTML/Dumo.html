Biography and Background

Dumo Manzini was born in Durban, South Africa, and attended high school in Pietermaritzburg. He developed a passion for leadership and entrepreneurship from a young age, often participating in school business competitions and community projects. He is currently studying Business Management because he aspires to become a successful entrepreneur and help small businesses grow in South Africa. Dumo believes that understanding business strategies, financial management, and marketing will enable him to start his own company one day. His long-term goal is to create job opportunities and contribute to economic development in his community.

Hobbies

Dumo Manzini is an active and adventurous person who enjoys staying engaged in both physical and creative activities. One of his biggest passions is playing soccer, where he often joins local weekend matches with friends. He enjoys the teamwork and strategy involved in the game, and it helps him stay fit and energetic. Apart from sports, Dumo has a deep interest in photography. He loves capturing moments of nature, city life, and people in their daily routines. He often spends his free time exploring new places, looking for the perfect shot that tells a story. Additionally, he enjoys reading business books and biographies of successful entrepreneurs, which inspire him to refine his own business ideas.
