Biography and Background

Thato Ngobese was born in Johannesburg, South Africa, and completed high school in Soweto. From a young age, Thato was always fascinated by how things worked and had a natural curiosity about technology. She is currently studying Computer Science because she has a passion for coding and software development. Thato dreams of building innovative applications that can improve everyday life. She is particularly interested in artificial intelligence and cybersecurity, as she believes the future relies heavily on technology-driven solutions.

Hobbies

Thato enjoys coding and developing small software projects in her free time. She is also a big fan of gaming, particularly strategy and simulation games that challenge her thinking. Additionally, she spends time learning about ethical hacking and cybersecurity.
