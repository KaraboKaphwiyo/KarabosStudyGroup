Biography and Background

Moeletsi Shabangu was born in Mbombela (Nelspruit), South Africa, and attended high school in Witbank. From a young age, he has been passionate about numbers, problem-solving, and financial literacy. He is currently studying Finance and Investment Management because he wants to understand how money works and help individuals and businesses make smart financial decisions. His dream is to become a financial analyst and eventually start his own investment firm. Moeletsi believes that financial education is key to building a successful future, and he hopes to teach others about smart money management..

Hobbies

Moeletsi enjoys stock market analysis and financial trading simulations. He is also an avid fan of chess, as he believes it sharpens his strategic thinking. In his free time, he participates in business competitions and finance workshops to enhance his investment skills.
